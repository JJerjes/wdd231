Website - Fisiocarrillo

1. Site Name

Name: Fisiocarrillo

Reason for Choice: The name "FisioCarrillo" clearly reflects the nature of the business, a center dedicated to physiotherapy and rehabilitation. The use of the last name "Carrillo" gives a personal and unique touch to the name

2. Purpose of the Site

The purpose of the FisioCarrillo website is to provide detailed information about the therapy and rehabilitation services offered by the center. The site will also serve to schedule appointments online, provide information about health insurance and offer educational resources for the well-being of patients.

3. Scenarios

  • What types of treatments do you offer at FisioCarrillo?
  • Do you accept health insurance? Which is it?

4. Color Scheme

The color scheme for the site will be as follows:

#19539b (for headers and footers)

#ddd (for the background of the site)

5. Typography

The main font used on the site will be'Montserrat', a modern and legible font, suitable for the clear presentation of health services.
